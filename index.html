Algorithm
noun · Computer Science
A step-by-step set of instructions that a computer follows to solve a problem or complete a task — like a recipe, but for machines. Every app you use runs on thousands of algorithms working together.
"Google's search algorithm processes billions of queries every second to find the most relevant results."
